About This Cause
Active

The Death Support Fund is the founding cause of Sylhet Welfare Trust, established in 2014. All active members contribute a fixed amount each month. When a member passes away, the accumulated funds are disbursed to their registered nominees to cover funeral costs, travel for family members, and immediate financial relief.
