Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time? +
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect from your next billing cycle. Downgrades will not delete your data — but feature access will be restricted to your new plan's limits.
Is there a contract or minimum term? +
No contracts. Monthly plans can be cancelled any time. Annual plans are billed upfront for 12 months but can be cancelled with a pro-rata refund within 30 days.
How is member data protected? +
All member data is encrypted at rest and in transit. KYC documents are stored in secure, access-controlled storage. We are GDPR compliant and never share member data with third parties.
Can multiple organisations share a plan? +
Professional plans support a single organisation. If you manage multiple organisations (e.g. a parent charity with local chapters), contact us for Enterprise pricing which supports multiple organisations under one account.
Do you support Zakat fund management specifically? +
Yes. Ummah Platform was designed with Zakat, Sadaqah, and Islamic welfare funds in mind. You can create Zakat-specific causes, set collection periods, and document distributions to eligible recipients (Asnaf).
Is there a free trial for Professional? +
Yes — the Professional plan includes a 30-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll have access to all Pro features during the trial period.
