Help & FAQ

Answers to common questions for members and administrators.

Getting Started

How do I join an organization?

Register an account, then use the Join Organization step during onboarding or request access from the Organization page.

How do I switch roles?

Use the role switcher in the top bar to view Admin, Treasurer, Auditor, or Member experiences.

Contributions & Receipts

How are receipts verified?

Receipts are reviewed by the Treasurer and marked approved or rejected. You will receive a notification when the status changes.

What payment methods are supported?

Bank transfer, cash, and mobile banking are supported in this prototype.

Security & Privacy

Is two-factor authentication available?

Yes. Admins can enable 2FA from the Security Settings page.

Where can I see audit logs?

Audit logs are under Reports & Audit in the sidebar.
