Built for Communities That Care

Ummah Platform was born from the real struggles of diaspora welfare organisations — managing funds on WhatsApp, tracking contributions in spreadsheets, and losing trust when things went wrong.

Our Story

From WhatsApp groups to a trusted platform

In 2019, a group of Bangladeshi community leaders in Birmingham were managing a welfare fund for 180+ families. Every month, money was collected via bank transfers, acknowledged on WhatsApp, and disbursed based on handwritten notes.

When a dispute arose over a death support payout, the lack of transparency nearly tore the community apart. That experience inspired Ummah Platform.

We built the system we wished we had — transparent, auditable, easy to use, and culturally appropriate for Muslim community organisations, welfare trusts, and diaspora support groups around the world.
