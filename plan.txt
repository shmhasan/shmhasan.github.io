OVERVIEW
--------
Build a fully navigable, polished, multi-page static HTML prototype simulating
a complete community fund management SaaS. No frameworks. Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS.
~70+ pages across public, onboarding, and app sections.

Delivery pattern: Files generated in batches, each batch self-contained.

DESIGN SYSTEM SUMMARY (guiding all batches)
--------------------------------------------
Color palette:
  Primary:    #1B4F72   (deep navy blue — trust)
  Accent:     #2E86AB   (teal blue — action)
  Success:    #27AE60   (green)
  Warning:    #F39C12   (amber)
  Danger:     #E74C3C   (red)
  Neutral:    #F4F6F9   (background)
  Text:       #2C3E50   (dark slate)
  Muted:      #7F8C8D   (secondary text)

Typography:
  Font stack: 'Inter', system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif
  (loaded from Google Fonts CDN in <head>)

Layout:
  - Left sidebar (260px) + topbar (64px) for app area
  - Full-width topnav for public pages
  - Max content width: 1200px

Status badge color map:
  pending       → amber background
  under_review  → blue background
  approved      → green background
  rejected      → red background
  verified      → teal background
  active        → green background
  suspended     → grey background
  published     → teal background
  closed        → grey background
  paid          → green background
  draft         → light grey background
