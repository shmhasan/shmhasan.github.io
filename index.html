Everything your community needs

From onboarding new members to disbursing emergency funds — Ummah Platform handles the full lifecycle with transparency and accountability.

👥

Member Management

Register members, manage roles, approve requests, verify identities via NID or passport, and track nominee relationships.

💚

Cause & Campaign Management

Create support causes — death support, medical emergencies, zakat, mosque renovation — and track contributions in real time.

💰

Contribution Tracking

Accept contributions by bank transfer, cash, or mobile banking. Members upload receipts; treasurers verify and approve.

📤

Disbursement Workflows

Request, approve, and record disbursements with a full audit trail. Supports multi-currency including BDT and GBP.

✅

KYC Verification

Verify members and nominees using NID, passport, or driving licence. Admin review queue with document upload support.

📈

Reports & Audit Logs

Full financial reports — collections, disbursements, cause-wise, member-wise — plus immutable audit logs for every action.

How it works

From setup to first disbursement in four simple steps.

🏢
Create Your Organisation
Register your trust, mosque committee, or welfare association. Invite admin and treasurer roles.
👥
Onboard Members
Members join via invite or request. Verify identities with NID or passport. Manage nominees.
💚
Run Causes
Create support causes, collect contributions, upload payment receipts, and track progress in real time.
📤
Disburse & Report
Approve disbursements, mark payments, and publish transparent reports for members and auditors.
